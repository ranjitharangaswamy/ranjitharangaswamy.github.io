About Me

Seasoned Litigator | Passionate about Justice and Legal Innovation

I am an advocate licensed to practice law in India since 2020. I graduated with a Bachelor in Arts and Bachelor in Law (Honours) professional degree from School of Law, CHRIST University , Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. I did my undergraduate dissertation thesis on Comparative analysis of Anti-suit injunctions across multiple jurisdictions.

I love exploring the interplay of law with other disciplines, such as user experience design and research, artificial intelligence, and technology policy.
